# Zombie vocal sources

All ten vocal files in this directory are derived from Creative Commons Zero (CC0 1.0) recordings.

- `vocal-01.ogg` through `vocal-06.ogg`: “Zomby sfx pack” by saturn91, downloaded from [OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/content/zomby-sfx-pack).
- `vocal-07.ogg` through `vocal-10.ogg`: “Undead Moans” by AntumDeluge, downloaded from [OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/content/undead-moans).

The game copies are mono 44.1 kHz Vorbis files normalized to -18 LUFS with a -2 dB true-peak ceiling. The source pages identify both packs as CC0; attribution is included here for provenance even though the license does not require it.
